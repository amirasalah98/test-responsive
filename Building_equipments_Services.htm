Our Services

The main activities of The Company are:

We have large collection of used heavy machinery and constructional plants for sales. Al Qudrah Used Building Machinery Trading is your one stop place for entire construction machinery requirement including new machinery and used machinery.

Hiring of Plants & Equipment for Construction Works:

Our hiring fleet includes Generators, Welding Machines, Air Compressors, Plate Compactor s, Jack Hammers, Power Floats, Concrete & Asphalt Cutting Machines, Water Pumps, Scaling Machines, Vibrators, Mixer Machines, Bobcats, Roller & Plate Compactors, Excavators & Wheel Loaders, Backhoe (JCB), Road Rollers, Water Trucks, Dump Trucks and Flat-Bed Trailers, motor Graders, Crane, etc

Handling of Construction Works:

We undertaking constructional works such as earth works, excavation work, soil improvement, watering, etc.

Transportation & Supply of Building materials & Water for Construction Works:

We have a highly appreciated transportation facility to supply building materials, water and other things.

Workshop Services & Generator Alternator Rewinding:

Our workshop is capable for all mechanical works. We are specialists in generator alternator rewinding.

Vehicle Evaluation:

We are evaluating vehicles throughout U. A. E. for Financial Companies and private parties. We are the main vehicle evaluators of some of the leading financial companies in U. A. E.
